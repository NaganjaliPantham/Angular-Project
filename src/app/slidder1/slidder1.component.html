Agriculture

Agriculture is the practice of cultivating crops and raising livestock, providing the food necessary to sustain human populations

Aquaculture

Aquaculture is the practice of cultivating aquatic organisms like fish, shellfish, and plants in controlled environments, essentially "farming in water" to produce food for human consumption

Apiculture

Apiculture, also known as beekeeping, is the practice of raising honeybee colonies to produce valuable products like honey and beeswax, while also contributing to pollination in the environment.

Dairy Farming

Dairy farming is the practice of raising livestock like cows and goats primarily to produce milk, which is then processed into various dairy products like cheese, yogurt, and butter.

Sericulture

Sericulture is the process of raising silkworms to produce silk. It's an agro-based cottage industry that involves cultivating food plants, rearing silkworms, and reeling cocoons.

Poultry Farming

Poultry farming is the practice of raising domesticated birds for food, such as eggs and meat. It involves providing the birds with food, water, shelter, and a safe environment.
